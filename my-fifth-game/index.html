    • The bird character moves via the arrow keys. The bird can move up, down, left, and right. It can also travel through the bounds of the game to come out the other side. The goal is grab the coconuts on the screen (you must hover directly over the center of the coconut). You die if your chain of birds touches itself. The bird also must avoid the poison apple and unfriendly peacock. Colliding with those makes you lose part of your chain. If you lose your entire chain, you lose too.

    • After flying for days through the desert, Tweety the bird stumbles upon a desert Oasis. As their flock joins them, they search for all the coconuts available in order to quench their thirst and fight their hunger. Unfortunately, each bird of the flock is getting very testy from hunger and does not want any of the other members of the flock to bother it while they search for food. Be careful to avoid touching other members of the flock! Also avoid the strange looking apple and the mean looking peacock. They might make you lose part of your new flock!
