Eight O'clock At The Oasis

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
    • The game has different background, characters, and music. There is also a score keeping aspect to it now so players can try to best their own scores.

  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • The coconuts appear at random throughout the screen, a song plays during the game, and there is a score board to keep track of your score. The game also restarts itself when you lose.

  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • I learned how to add music to the game and how to create objects at random throughout the screen. The hardest part was trying to get all the characters to show on the screen correctly and at the correct times.
